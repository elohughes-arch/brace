How it works

From your glasses to your stats.

Three steps — and the only one you do is the shooting.

01

Record with your glasses

Press the button on your Meta smart glasses and shoot. Hands-free POV capture from your own eyeline — no rig to carry, nothing to aim but your gun.

02

Brace processes your day

Open the app and process the session. Brace reads every second of footage and finds the shooting — each target, each hit and miss, your timing and your mount.

03

Review your metrics & footage

Your numbers update and your footage is ready. Play any shot back, see where the day was won or lost, and shoot it better next time.

How the metrics are recorded: Brace reads your POV footage frame by frame — detecting the moment of each shot, the target, the break and your timing — so the numbers come from what actually happened, not what you remember.
